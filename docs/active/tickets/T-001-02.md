## Context

The sky is the top 35% of every frame — a vertical gradient from near-black zenith to a repo-hued horizon. The sun/moon disc sits above-right of the horizon in the complement hue. At VelocityDemon tier, the sky hue rotates.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] `draw_sky` fills the sky region with a smooth vertical gradient from `SKY_ZENITH` (#05050f) to a horizon color derived from `RepoSeed::accent_hue`
- [ ] Gradient uses correct HSL→RGB conversion
- [ ] At VelocityDemon tier, sky hue rotates at 1°/s using `world.time`
- [ ] `draw_sun` renders a filled circle at `(pixel_w * 0.72, horizon_y - 40)` in complement hue
- [ ] Sun radius pulses ±3px at rate `(commits_per_min * 0.4).min(3.0)` Hz
- [ ] 6px bloom bleed of road accent color at bottom of sky region connecting road to horizon
- [ ] HSL helper functions are correct and tested
