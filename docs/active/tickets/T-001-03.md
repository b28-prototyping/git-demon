## Context

The road is the visual centerpiece — a pseudo-perspective trapezoid filling the bottom 65% of the frame. Each scanline maps to a depth value; road width interpolates from narrow at horizon to wide at camera. The road curves based on a `curve_offset` value that shifts the center quadratically with depth.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] `draw_road` renders the full road region with correct perspective (narrow at top, wide at bottom)
- [ ] Alternating stripe colors swap based on `world.z_offset / depth` for forward motion illusion
- [ ] Rumble strips (12px each side) alternate between white and red with stripes
- [ ] Verge regions outside rumble strips use alternating green tones
- [ ] Road center shifts by `curve_offset * depth²` creating perspective-correct curves
- [ ] `draw_grid` renders thin neon horizontal lines at perspective-projected Z intervals in accent hue at alpha 150
- [ ] Grid lines follow the curve offset
- [ ] At VelocityDemon: `ROAD_MAX_HALF` expands 5%, horizon drops 2%
- [ ] `horizon_ratio()` and `road_max_half()` correctly adjust for VelocityDemon tier
